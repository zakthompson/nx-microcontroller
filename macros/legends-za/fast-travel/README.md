# Fast Travel Bot

This bot simply fast travels to same location over and over again to respawn all Pok√©mon within a 50m radius.

## How to Use

1. Make sure your map is zoomed in as far as possible (Open the map and press the right stick up a couple times)
2. Fast travel to your chosen location
3. Plug in the bot

## Requirements

- The destination location must be unlocked in your game

## Tips

- If the bot isn't properly hitting the target, double-check that you have zoomed the map in all the way
